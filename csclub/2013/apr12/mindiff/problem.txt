You have swallowed a stick of length L and you have to locate this stick.
First, you stretch out the digestive tract.
Next, you measure the vertical displacement at N equidistant points.
You need to output the minimum range of all slices of L consecutive elements.
Input:
N L
{N lines, 1 number each}
Output:
D
Sample input:
10 5
1
-1
3
2
2
3
2
2
3
0
Sample output:
1
Explanation:
The sequence starting with the first 3 ranges from 2 to 3, so the range is 1.
