You are given an array of N integers. Your goal is to find the length L of the longest subarray with the minimum and maximum elements differing by at most D.
Input:
N D
{N integers}
(repeats until N=0)
Output:
(for each test case)
L
Sample input:
5 2
1
2
3
4
1
Sample output:
3
Explanation: [1,2,3],4,1 has difference 2 and length 3. There is another subarray, 1,[2,3,4],1, and it also has length 3.
