Lines of symmetry are lines that a figure can be reflected over without changing the figure. Print the number of lines of symmetry in each figure. If it's infinite, print "inf".
Input:
H W
H*{
  W characters ("*" or ".")
}
{repeats until H*W=0}
Sample input:
3 2
..
..
..
2 1
*
*
4 5
..*..
.***.
*****
*...*
3 3
.*.
***
.*.
4 4
.*..
...*
*...
..*.
Sample output:
inf
2
1
4
4
